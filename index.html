  • Indian currency is made up of ₹ (Rupees ) and paise  of following kinds:  50p, 100p,  1 ₹ , 2 ₹ , 5₹ , 10 ₹ 50 ₹ , 100 ₹ , 500₹ and 1000₹ It is possible to make 20000 ₹ in the following way:  2 x 50 p + 9 x 100p + 1000x19 ₹ + 500 x 1 ₹ + 4 x 100 ₹ + 10 x 9 ₹ How many different ways can 20000 ₹  be made using any number of coins?
